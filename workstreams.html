Home / Workstreams

Six workstreams, one investment-ready corridor.

Each workstream reduces a specific category of uncertainty. Together they move the corridor from concept to a sequenced, fundable, permittable implementation path — with records to match.

1

Site readiness

Document parcel conditions, utility needs, transportation access, reuse constraints, and the permitting path for each authorized site.

Parcel conditions Utilities Transportation Permitting path

Records live in

  • Site files for Coleman and Flint (site profiles)
  • Regulatory and permitting folder with status, authority, and missing-document tracking
  • Permit register template for consistent tracking
2

Feedstock validation

Confirm available material streams — tires, rail ties, biomass, industrial materials — plus logistics assumptions, storage needs, and partner commitments.

Material streams Logistics Storage Partner commitments

Records live in

  • Feedstock and materials sections of each site file
  • Source register entries for every partner-provided document
  • Assumptions register rows with confidence and verification dates
3

Environmental method

Build defensible baselines and emissions-accounting methods suitable for funders and regulators. Methods are documented for review — outcomes are never claimed without cited sources.

Baselines GHG methodology Regulator-ready

Records live in

  • Regulatory and permitting folder for environmental review materials
  • Source register for methodology references and datasets
  • Risk register entries for methodology and compliance risks
4

Community engagement

Create plain-language materials, meeting records, feedback loops, and host-community commitments — with approved outreach language only.

Plain language Meeting records Feedback loops

Records live in

  • Engagement folder: plans, outreach schedules, approved talking points
  • Community benefits folder and benefit trackers (community page)
  • Governed meeting notes from the standard template
5

Finance and governance

Map grants, tax tools, private capital, operating agreements, and accountability controls without overstating eligibility, likelihood, or commitments.

Grants Tax tools Private capital Controls

Records live in

  • Funding landscape tracker (funding page)
  • Grant submissions working areas by program
  • Controlled investor data room, where authorized
6

Implementation roadmap

Translate planning into a sequenced path for engineering, permitting, procurement, and operations — including role-level training and continuity through personnel turnover.

Sequencing Training design Operator onboarding Continuity

Records live in

  • Implementation and training folder with schedule tracking
  • Deliverables tracker with owners, due dates, and approvals
  • Decision log entries for sequencing decisions
How work moves

Every deliverable follows the same lifecycle.

Plan

Scope the deliverable from a template with an owner and due date.

Source

Register the documents that support every claim it makes.

Draft

Build the material with placeholders where sources are pending.

Route

Review and route for the documented approval authority.

Record

Log decisions, changes, and final storage location.
